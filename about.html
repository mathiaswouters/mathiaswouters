About Me

I'm Mathias Wouters, a Linux System Engineer based in Belgium with a Professional Bachelor's in Cloud & Cyber Security. I'm currently working at PSA Antwerp, where I specialize in Linux systems, automation, and DevOps practices.

Alongside my engineering work, I develop SaaS products with a focus on scalability, performance, and solving real business challenges. My projects bring together modern web technologies and enterprise-grade infrastructure practices, bridging technical expertise with product development.

My long-term ambition is to grow a portfolio of SaaS solutions and build technology-driven businesses that deliver lasting impact.

About Me

I'm Mathias Wouters, a 23-year-old Linux System Engineer based in Belgium with a Professional Bachelor's in Cloud & Cyber Security. Currently working at PSA Antwerp, I specialize in Linux systems, automation, and cloud technologies.

My passion lies in building robust, scalable infrastructure that mirrors production environments. Through my extensive homelab setup, I continuously expand my expertise in DevOps, Kubernetes, and modern infrastructure patterns.

Beyond infrastructure, I'm actively developing multiple SaaS products using modern web technologies. My focus is on creating scalable, fast applications that solve real business problems and generate sustainable revenue. I'm particularly interested in B2B tools and productivity solutions.

I have strong interests in finance, investing, and business strategy. Living at home allows me to save aggressively while reinvesting in my SaaS ventures. My goal is to build a portfolio of profitable SaaS products and eventually transition to full-time entrepreneurship, potentially relocating to North America for greater market opportunities.
